More than 30 Years of Experience

Alrashed Egyptian Company, established in 1985, is a leading company in harvesting, packing, and exporting premium fruits and vegetables to global markets. Our work includes harvesting crops to ensure the selection of high quality crops.

Through our processing facility, we sort, prepare, and pack our products to meet the highest international standards. This dedication to quality and efficiency allows us to serve importers and wholesalers in markets worldwide, providing them with, high quality products.
